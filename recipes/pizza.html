Pizza

A delicious homemade pizza

This recipe will teach you how to easily make a delicious homemade pizza. It may seem like a lot of work, but the results promise a delicious meal and the satisfaction of mastering a kitchen staple. So, let's begin by listing the ingredients we need!

Ingredients

  • Yeast (1 standard packet)
  • Water (1 cup)
  • Flour (30 dag)
  • Oil (3-4 tbsp)
  • Salt (1 pinch)
  • Sugar (1 tbsp)
  • Cornmeal
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Dissolve your yeast in water
  2. Pour the mixture to the bowl with flour
  3. Add oil, salt and sugar
  4. Mix and knead
  5. Wait around 40 minutes until your dough rises (to make it rise faster store your dough in a warm place)
  6. Punch and shape your pizza, add cornmeal to your tray to make your crust more unique
  7. Smear a bit of oil on top of your dough
  8. Add the desired toppings and let your dough rise for another 15 minutes
  9. Bake in 230 degrees Celcius for 25 minutes
  10. Voila! Enjoy your pizza!
