Spaghetti bolognese

A mouth-watering homemade spaghetti bolognese

This recipe will teach you how to make this delicious and easy classic Italian dish. This pasta bolognese recipe is sure to become a family favourite.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Beef mince (400 g)
  • One onion
  • Two garlic cloves
  • Tomato sauce
  • Herbs and spices (mainly oregano, basil, salt, pepper)
  • Spaghetti pasta

Steps

  1. Prepare your tomatoe sauce by adding your spices
  2. Smash your garlic cloves and chop your onion and add it to the sauce
  3. Add olive oil to your pan and startd cooking your meat
  4. When it gets slightly brown add your tomatoe sauce and stew it for around 20 minutes
  5. In the meantime prepare your pasta by boiling it
  6. After your meat and sauce are ready apply it to pasta
  7. Enjoy your meal!
