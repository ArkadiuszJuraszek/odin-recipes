Lasagna

A tasty homemade lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too. Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic (two cloves)
  • Crushed tomatoes (1 can)
  • Tomato sauce (2 cans)
  • Tomato paste (2cans)
  • Sugar (2 tbsp)
  • Seasonings and spices (parsley, basil leaves, salt, balck pepper)
  • Sheets of lasagna noodles
  • Mozaarella, parmesan, ricota
  • One egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Create cheese mixture
  4. Start layering your lasagna and applying both the meat and the cheese mix on the sheets
  5. When layering apply in order: meat sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, mozarella slices, meatsauce and parmesan cheese
  6. Cover your lasagna in foil
  7. Bake it for about 50 minutes in 200 degrees Celcius
  8. After 25 minutes of baking take the foil cover off
  9. Let the lasagna rest
  10. Serve and enjoy your meal!
